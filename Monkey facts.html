Monkey facts

Check out some cool facts about monkeys!

  • Monkeys can understand basic sign language.
  • Some monkeys have tails that can grab things, like an extra hand!
  • Monkeys live in social groups called troops.
  • Capuchin monkeys are one of the smartest monkey species.
  • Monkeys love bananas, but they also eat a variety of fruits, leaves, and insects!
